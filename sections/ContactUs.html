Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +92 315 6547393, using the online booking form, or visiting our clinic during working hours.

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Please contact us directly to discuss insurance options and coverage details for your specific plan.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your ID, any dental insurance information, and a list of current medications or medical conditions.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, we provide emergency dental care. Please call us immediately if you have a dental emergency.

How can I pay for my treatment?

We accept cash, credit cards, and bank transfers. Payment plans may be available for certain treatments.
